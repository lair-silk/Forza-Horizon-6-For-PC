**What exactly is Forza Horizon 6?**
Forza Horizon 6 is a premium open-world racing and simulation-arcade experience developed by Playground Games and published by Xbox Game Studios. This edition shifts the world-famous automotive festival to a highly detailed representation of Japan, introducing complex urban racing networks, massive mountain drift environments, and deeper mechanical customization systems than ever before.


**Is Forza Horizon 6 playable on PlayStation 5?**
Yes. Microsoft has officially confirmed that Forza Horizon 6 is launching on the PlayStation 5 platform. While the Xbox Series X/S and PC versions launched in mid-2026, the PS5 edition is scheduled for its own targeted release window later within the annual calendar cycle.


**How does the map size compare to previous entries?**
This represents the largest and most dense map environment ever engineered for a Forza Horizon title. Beyond its overall physical size, the integration of Tokyo introduces immense verticality, highway routing, and complex multi-tier city blocks that radically increase total drivable surface area.


**Why is it exclusive to current-generation gaming hardware?**
By leaving legacy cross-generation consoles behind, the developers completely unshackled the graphics rendering pipelines. This allows the game engine to execute real-time ray-traced global illumination, ultra-dense urban traffic, zero-latency fast travel tracking, and immense level-of-detail ranges without structural performance drops.

The Standard edition grants access to the base game and pre-order car bonuses. Upgrading to the Premium or Deluxe bundles adds full access to the Car Pass, VIP membership multipliers, early access launch windows, and planned future map expansions right out of the box.


**Can you play the entire campaign mode in co-op with friends?**
Yes. The entire campaign framework—from initial tourist qualifiers to high-wristband legend events—is completely playable alongside friends via seamless drop-in/drop-out online convoy connectivity.


**How does the new "Estate" feature function?**
The Estate serves as a dedicated sandbox valley region in rural Japan. Unlike standard player houses, it gives you a massive, blank open-world canvas where you can build, place objects, and design custom properties or track networks alongside your friends.
